This is intended as, essentially, a content aggregation site for pathfinder. Choose a class, a race, some things you want to do, and it'll recommend potentially useful things while excluding things that don't work with what you're doing.

It's aimed at people who enjoy the room for expression in character creation, but don't want to trawl through thousands of useless feats to do it.
